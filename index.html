Training plan

Lifting · 3x week

  • Full body, Mon / Wed / Sat
  • Block 1 (wks 1–4): straight sets, 10 moves/day
  • Block 2 (wks 5–9): supersets, full arm/delt block
  • Block 3 (wk 10+): built from real data
  • Leave 3–4 reps in tank every set
  • Start at 60% of remembered loads

Swimming · 4x week

  • Tue / Thu / Fri + one weekend day
  • Week 1: 100m sets, 500m total
  • Month 2: 150m sets, 600m total
  • Month 4–6: 1km continuous target
  • Year 1 north star: 1.5km in 45 min
  • Lift first on double session days

The rules that make it stick

When Wednesday night is hard, read this

  • The training did its job last time. The diet is the variable.
  • A bad week doesn't end the cycle. One session does.
  • Feeling good in week 2 is the trap. Move to Block 2 on the calendar.
  • The belly goes last. Everything else improves first.
  • Don't track everything — track the wins that feel good.
  • 30-min swim still counts. Something beats nothing every time.
  • Late nights during the window are planned, not failure.
  • Genuinely fit in 12 months. Super lean is year two.
